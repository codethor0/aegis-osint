## Risks & Mitigation

### Identified Risks

1. **Resource Validation**
   - Risk: Invalid or malicious resources
   - Mitigation: Strict validation process, automated checks

2. **Legal Compliance**
   - Risk: Resources violating legal guidelines
   - Mitigation: Legal review process, compliance checklist

3. **Performance Degradation**
   - Risk: Slower performance with more resources
   - Mitigation: Performance testing, optimization, caching

4. **Scope Creep**
   - Risk: Features expanding beyond timeline
   - Mitigation: Strict prioritization, phased approach
